Research and Projects

  • [2024-2025] AI-Powered Weed Detection and GIS Mapping for Targeted Spraying
    • Developed and validated a computer vision pipeline processing high-resolution drone orthomosaic imagery to detect weeds and generate precise GIS coordinates, supporting autonomous sprayer tractors in performing targeted herbicide application.
    • Presented at the 2025 ASABE Annual International Meeting (AIM). This work fulfills the AI-based sensing and edge-deployment stages of my proposed research endeavor in precision agriculture — enabling scalable, data-driven weed management while reducing chemical usage and environmental impact.

  • [2025-2026] Autonomous Manipulation for Robotic Apple Harvesting
    • Extending my prior work in AI-based crop sensing toward autonomous physical interaction with crops: developed a simulation-based framework for planning and executing collision-free trajectories for a UR5 robotic arm in autonomous apple harvesting.
    • Built RRT/RRT-Connect modules with collision checking, reachability analysis, and visibility constraints, integrated in ROS and PyBullet with full environment setup, kinematics, and testing routines. This represents the integration stage of my proposed endeavor — moving from AI-based crop sensing toward AI-guided autonomous crop interaction and maintenance, consistent with the autonomous agricultural management system described in my research proposal.
    • Status: In progress.
